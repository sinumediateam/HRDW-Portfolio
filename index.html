About

HRW · LA

I'm Harrison — a Los Angeles–based senior editor, producer and director who treats generative AI as another lens on the camera rig. The work moves between commercial polish and experimental image-making.

Over the past decade the toolkit has grown from timelines and grade panels to diffusion models and latent video. I direct, cut and produce across commercials, corporate marketing, music videos, documentary, narrative comedy, product and event promos, real estate and sports media — and increasingly fuse traditional footage with AI-generated frames into something that couldn't exist any other way.
